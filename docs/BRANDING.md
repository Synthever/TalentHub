# Branding & Design Guide

## 1. Concept & Value Proposition
- **Tagline:** "Matching Talent with Logic"
- **Brand Vibe:** Professional, Tech-forward, Intelligent, Clean.

## 2. Design Guidelines (Stitch)
- **Primary Color:** Deep Blue (#1E3A8A) - Melambangkan kepercayaan/integritas.
- **Secondary Color:** AI Purple (#7C3AED) - Melambangkan inovasi & AI.
- **Typography:** Inter atau Montserrat (Modern Sans-serif).

## 3. UI/UX Focus
- Dashboard harus bersih (*minimalist*).
- Highlight utama pada *Match Score* (menggunakan *progress bar* atau *gauge chart*).
